Our Spaces

Explore Hope Station

Three distinct spaces, endless possibilities.

Space 01

The Garden Boardroom

A professional meeting space surrounded by nature. Natural light flows in, the garden provides a living backdrop, and the contained setting helps teams focus without distraction.

Configurations

  • Boardroom style: Up to 20 people
  • Classroom style: Up to 30 people
  • U-shape: Up to 24 people
  • Theatre style: Up to 40 people

Included

  • Presentation screen and display
  • High-speed wifi with backup power
  • Complimentary tea, coffee and water
  • Flipchart, whiteboard and markers
  • Secure on-site parking

Space 02

The Wellness Space

A restorative environment perfect for yoga, breathwork, sound healing, meditation and therapeutic sessions. The garden atmosphere and natural acoustics create a container for deep work and healing.

Ideal For

  • Yoga classes and workshops
  • Breathwork and sound healing sessions
  • Meditation mornings
  • Trauma-informed therapeutic groups
  • Wellness retreats and half-day workshops

Configuration

  • Circle / open floor: Up to 25 people
  • Workshop style: Up to 20 people
Book This Space
Space 03

The Garden Breakout

An outdoor extension of the indoor spaces. Use the garden for tea breaks, small group discussions, creative brainstorming, or as a breathing space between sessions. It's the feature that sets Hope Station apart from every other venue in George.

Features

  • Lush, maintained garden with seating areas
  • Natural shade and open-air sections
  • Private and enclosed within the property
  • Available to all bookings at no extra cost
