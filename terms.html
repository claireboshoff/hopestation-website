Venue Hire Terms & Conditions

Please read carefully before submitting your booking enquiry.

These terms and conditions ("Agreement") constitute a legally binding contract between Hope Station ("the Venue") and the client ("the Hirer") for the hire of the venue space. By submitting a booking enquiry and paying the deposit, you agree to all terms below.

1. Booking & Confirmation

1.1. A booking is only confirmed upon receipt of a signed agreement (or digital acceptance) and a 50% non-refundable deposit of the total hire fee.

1.2. The Venue will confirm availability within 24 hours of receiving an enquiry.

1.3. The remaining 50% balance is due no later than 48 hours before the event date.

1.4. Failure to pay the balance by the due date may result in cancellation of the booking.

2. Payment

2.1. All prices are quoted in South African Rand (ZAR) and are inclusive of VAT where applicable.

2.2. Accepted payment methods: EFT (bank transfer), credit/debit card (via PayFast), and instant EFT.

2.3. A refundable damage deposit of R2,000 is required for private events and will be refunded within 7 business days of the event, subject to inspection.

3. Cancellation Policy

3.1. 14+ days before event: Full refund of deposit (less R500 administration fee).

3.2. 7 to 14 days before event: 50% of deposit refunded.

3.3. Less than 7 days before event: No refund. Full amount payable.

3.4. Cancellations must be submitted in writing via email to info@hopestation.co.za.

3.5. The Venue reserves the right to cancel a booking due to force majeure, in which case a full refund will be provided.

4. Venue Use & Time Restrictions

4.1. Hope Station is located within Minnesota House, a rehabilitation facility. All hirers and guests must respect the environment and its residents at all times.

4.2. Weekday bookings: 08:00 – 16:00 (corporate).

4.3. Evening bookings: 17:00 – 21:00 (wellness/events).

4.4. Weekend bookings: 08:00 – 18:00 (events/workshops).

4.5. All events must conclude and all guests must vacate the premises by the agreed end time. Overtime will be charged at R500 per additional hour.

4.6. The Venue reserves the right to designate blackout dates where no external bookings will be accepted.

5. Alcohol Policy

5.1. Alcohol is strictly prohibited at Hope Station unless prior written approval has been granted by the Venue management.

5.2. This policy is non-negotiable and exists to protect the rehabilitation environment.

5.3. Any breach of this policy will result in immediate termination of the event, forfeiture of all fees, and forfeiture of the damage deposit.

6. Noise & Conduct

6.1. The Hirer is responsible for the behaviour and conduct of all guests.

6.2. Excessive noise, disruptive behaviour, or any activity that disturbs the rehabilitation environment will not be tolerated.

6.3. The Venue reserves the right to terminate any event immediately if these conditions are breached, without refund.

7. Capacity

7.1. Maximum venue capacity is 40 persons (depending on configuration).

7.2. The Hirer must not exceed the agreed number of guests. The Venue reserves the right to refuse entry to additional guests beyond the agreed capacity.

8. Damage & Liability

8.1. The Hirer is liable for any damage to the Venue, its fixtures, fittings, gardens, or equipment caused by the Hirer or their guests during the event.

8.2. The cost of repair or replacement will be deducted from the damage deposit. If the damage exceeds the deposit, the Hirer is liable for the balance.

8.3. The Venue is not responsible for any loss of, or damage to, the Hirer's or guests' personal property.

8.4. The Venue is not liable for any injury sustained by the Hirer or their guests on the premises, except where caused by the Venue's negligence.

9. Catering & External Suppliers

9.1. Complimentary tea, coffee and water are provided with every booking.

9.2. External catering may be arranged through the Venue's approved suppliers at an additional cost.

9.3. The Hirer may not bring external catering without prior written approval from the Venue.

10. Setup & Cleanup

10.1. The Venue will provide the agreed seating configuration before the event.

10.2. The Hirer must leave the space in the same condition as received. Additional cleaning fees may apply if the space is left in an unreasonable state.

11. Photography & Media

11.1. The Hirer may take photographs at the Venue for personal and professional use.

11.2. The Hirer grants the Venue permission to use photographs from the event for marketing purposes, unless the Hirer explicitly opts out in writing before the event.

12. Compliance

12.1. This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.

12.2. The Hirer must comply with all applicable laws, including the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008, the Occupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993, and relevant municipal bylaws.

12.3. The Hirer is responsible for obtaining any permits or licenses required for their specific event.

13. Indemnity

13.1. The Hirer indemnifies and holds harmless Hope Station, Minnesota House, and their owners, employees and agents from any claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from the Hirer's use of the Venue.

14. Amendments

14.1. The Venue reserves the right to amend these terms and conditions at any time. The terms applicable at the time of booking will apply to that booking.

By submitting a booking enquiry and paying the deposit, you confirm that you have read, understood and accepted these terms and conditions.
