Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?+

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for weekday events and 4 weeks for weekend events. Popular months (October to December) fill up quickly, so book early for year-end functions.

What is the cancellation policy?+

Full refund for cancellations 14+ days before the event. 50% refund for 7-14 days notice. No refund for cancellations less than 7 days before the event. See our full terms for details.

Is alcohol allowed?+

Hope Station operates within a rehabilitation facility environment. Alcohol is strictly prohibited unless prior written approval has been granted. This is non-negotiable and part of our venue hire agreement.

Do you provide catering?+

Complimentary tea, coffee and water are included with every booking. Full catering (lunch, snacks, platters) is available as an add-on from R120 per person. We work with trusted local caterers in George.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept EFT, credit/debit card payments and instant EFT via our secure online payment system. A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due 48 hours before the event.
