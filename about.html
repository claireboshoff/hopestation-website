About Us

The Story Behind Hope Station

More than a venue. A space with intention, atmosphere and purpose.

Our Story

Born from Purpose

Hope Station is situated inside Minnesota House, a rehabilitation facility in George, Western Cape. But it operates as its own independent space — a venue that draws from the restorative energy of its surroundings while serving the broader community.

The name "Hope Station" reflects what happens here: people arrive carrying weight and leave lighter. Whether it's a corporate team finding alignment, a wellness practitioner creating sacred space, or a speaker sharing their journey — this is where transformation begins.

Our lush garden setting, secure grounds and intentional atmosphere make this unlike any other venue in the Garden Route. This isn't a hotel function room. It's a space designed to help people think, heal and connect.

What Defines Us

Our Values

Intentionality

Every event at Hope Station is hosted with purpose. We don't do volume — we do meaning. Every detail is considered, from the setup to the atmosphere.

Safety

Our space exists within a recovery environment. We take the safety, privacy and comfort of everyone on the property seriously. Clear boundaries protect everyone.

Restoration

Whether you're here for business or healing, the garden setting, natural light and calm atmosphere are designed to restore — not drain. You'll leave clearer than you arrived.

The Setting

George, Garden Route

George is the fastest-growing city in South Africa and the heart of the Garden Route. With its mountains, forests and mild climate, it's the ideal destination for offsites, retreats and intentional gatherings. Hope Station puts you in the middle of it — a garden sanctuary with easy access from the George Airport (10 minutes) and the N2.
